GAMES

Ideal for fans of:
• The Blair Witch Project, REC, and V/H/S
• Found footage genre

• Paranormal videos on the internet
• Urban legends

EXHUME MICRO RPG

EXHUME is a rules light fantasy role-playing game. It was designed with new players in mind and a fast-paced, free play style. It's also my attempt to bring some of the "new school revolution" style to the gaming table.


Some of my reasons for creating EXHUME:

  • I wanted to have a small game system that I could carry around, and play anywhere using just a couple of d6.
  • A game system simple enough to keep recruiting new members for my gaming group.
  • Quick character creation, but truly, really fast.
  • An elf game that not only had combat rules.
  • A set of rules that could be adaptable for use with any adventure.

ULTRA VIOLENCE RPG

A love letter to 90's FPS games; A role-playing game about Space Marines condemned to crush hordes of flesh and bone of demonic enemies. All with a unique, brutal, and violent combat system.

The game is currently in development. Art and layout by Penny Melgarejo and writing by Irvin Morales.

KOBOLDCON

The first convention dedicated to indie and old-school tabletop role-playing games in Mexico.

Role Per Second is a co-creator and co-organizer of KoboldCon; the first convention dedicated to indie and old-school tabletop role-playing games in the country. A landmark for an alternative and analog Geek culture.

KoboldCon was created to hold gaming sessions and promote the hobby. During its events, the RPG enthusiast community gathers to share gaming tables, drinks, and some good moments. Additionally, it aims to promote the independent gaming movement through the sale and promotion of various related items.

ABOUT IRVIN MORALES

AUTHOR, DESIGNER AND CONTENT CREATOR

Irvin Morales is a tabletop gaming enthusiast, especially fond of role-playing games. He began playing around 2003 and hasn't stopped since. Telling strange, unsettling, and extraordinary stories became his passion.

He has worked professionally in the tabletop gaming industry since 2017, building and promoting various projects and movements, such as El Reino de los Juegos de Mesa, one of the largest hobby stores in the country. He also organized some of the country's first specialized events, such as Free RPG Day 2017, International Tabletop Day 2017, and his own event, Guadalajara Tabletop Convention 2019.

Currently, Irvin is working full-time writing, designing, and doing layout for independent role-playing games. He is also building a highly creative player community on his Patreon, and directing the specialized in-person role-playing event, which he co-organizes with The Death Die Club: KoboldCon.
